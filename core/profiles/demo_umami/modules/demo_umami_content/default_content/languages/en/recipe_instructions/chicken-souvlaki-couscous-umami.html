For the chicken:

  1. Cut the chicken into approximately 1 inch pieces, 16 pieces will provide 4 pieces per skewer. Place the chicken into a large mixing bowl.
  2. Add the yogurt, dried mint, smoked paprika, dried oregano, ground cinnamon, lemon zest and juice, olive oil, salt, and pepper to the bowl.
  3. Thoroughly mix the ingredients in the bowl.
  4. Leave in the fridge to marinate for at least 2 hours. If you have the time, the chicken will taste even better if left to marinate in the fridge overnight.
  5. When ready to cook, preheat the oven to 375°F/190°C.
  6. Using 4 skewers, carefully pierce the chicken onto each skewer spreading the pieces evenly.
  7. Place the chicken skewers onto a baking tray and cook in the middle of the oven for 20 minutes or until the chicken is cooked through. Check the chicken is cooked through by cutting into one of the pieces with a sharp knife, the chicken is cooked when it is white all through.
  8. While the chicken is cooking, prepare the couscous.

For the couscous:

  1. Pour 250g of couscous into a medium to a large mixing bowl.
  2. Add 1 tsp of dried mint and mix the couscous and mint.
  3. Pour 350ml of boiling water into the bowl and mix.
  4. Cover and leave to stand for at least 5 minutes.

To serve:

  1. Add the couscous to a suitable serving dish.
  2. Place the chicken skewers on top of the couscous and squeeze some lemon juice over the dish.
